Bankala Kuura Recipe

Ingredients

  • Banka (Tree Gum)
  • Jeera (Cumin Seeds)
  • Curry Leaves
  • Chili Powder
  • Salt
  • Mustard Seeds
  • Oil
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Coconut Powder
  • Coriander Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Water (for cooking)

Instructions

  1. First, wash and cut the Bankas (Brinjals) into small cubes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add cumin seeds (jeera) and curry leaves to the oil, and sauté for a minute.
  4. Now, add the chopped Bankas (tree gum) and brinjal cubes to the pan and sauté for 5-7 minutes until they turn soft.
  5. Add turmeric powder, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix everything well.
  6. Add coconut powder and garam masala. Stir and cook for another 3-5 minutes.
  7. Add a little water to help the curry cook. Cover and cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Once the Bankas are fully cooked and the curry thickens, remove it from the heat.
  9. Serve hot with rice or roti.
